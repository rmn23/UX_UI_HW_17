Ruth-Makaita Nhera

I am a graduate UX/UI designer. I specialise in UX Research and Prototyping and enjoy the process of pairing together user and company needs in order to create an impactful product.

Memories Journalling App

I designed a journalling app for users who wanted a journalling app with a wellbeing aspect alongside two other UX Designers. This app achieved the goal of providing a space for users to journal consistently through notifications and a significant focus on wellbeing through daily affirmations and a reflective question of the day.

Travel Assured App Design

I designed an app for people who frequently travel post pandemic that ensured all travel requirements were up to date and easy to read. I used InVision and Figma to build my prototype.

Canada Border Agency Website

I redesigned the CBSA website focusing on the navigation as users found the current site to be dull and confusing. I used Figma to build my prototype.
